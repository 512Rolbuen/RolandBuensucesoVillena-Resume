I am Roland Buensuceso-Villena, I am 26 Years Old, and a graduate of the University of California, Riverside with a Bachelors in Economics/Administrative Studies, and a graduate of the University of Southern California, with a Masters in Public Administration. Despite having graduated from University with my masters only three years ago, I have accumulated a total of four years of increasingly responsible local government experience, ranging from working at the county library to working as a Management Analyst for a city of 76,000 people in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am also a licensed Certified Public Accountant under the State of California.

Accounting, finance, and local government work has always been my passion, and I have worked to familiarize myself with not only California local government finance regulations, but also local government rules in general. I continue to keep up to date on the latest rule changes, as well as spend my spare time learning more about California’s intricate but fascinating local government regulatory structure. It is my hope that new job experiences only further my knowledge of such, as well as help me develop new skills to master.
